Our Services

Powered with decades of experience

Corporate / Business

When it comes to starting a new venture, expanding, buying, selling, we’re pros at legal obligations and formalities. With us, you’ll move with ease may it be formations and acquisitions or mergers and sales.

Franchises

If you’re looking to move to the U.S. with your Foreign brand, or, buy into an existing Franchise, we’ll handle your concerns from A-Z. If you’re looking to move to India, Europe or the Middle East, we can assist you to get there!

Real Estate

We’ve got massive experience in negotiating, drafting, reviewing and transacting, legal documentation. We’ll do the due diligence concerned with acquiring that dream property, may it be selling, leasing, financing or investing & managing.

Entertainment / Media

From startups to growing businesses, we’ll walk you through everything from setup, to expansion, production, licenses, contracts, distribution, acquisitions and sales.

Litigation / Mediation

Disputes are bound to happen, but when you have us backing you, we’ll help you win them, resolve them or walk away hassle-free.

Matrimonial

We’re reputed for our advice, backed with a clear strategy, so we’ve got you covered from contested and uncontested matrimonial proceedings to property division and distribution disputes.

Intellectual Property

Your Ideas cannot be protected unless, we file Copyrights, Trademarks or Patents to protect them. Let’s ensure your next great innovation, script, music, film or software is protected by registering your rights.

International Consulting

Confused? Need advice on cross-border, structuring, operations, funding, real estate, management, employment, taxes, and immigration? We’ll advise you and connect you to the experts.

Software / IT / Patents

Whether you’re a service provider or a products solution, developing, licensing, innovative, disrupting, sourcing, or a platform, in the USA or in India, we will guide and protect you.
